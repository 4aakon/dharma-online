Privacy Policy for Dharma Online

Last updated: July 2026

Dharma Online ("the app") helps people find publicly listed online Buddhist teachings and events. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

What we collect

The app itself does not require you to create an account, and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or location, in order to browse events.

If you choose to add an event to your own calendar, that action happens through your device's own calendar app and is not seen or stored by us.

Timezone detection

The app detects your device's timezone locally, in your browser, purely to display event times correctly. This information is never sent to or stored on any server.

Event data sources

Event listings shown in the app are gathered from public sources, including public Telegram channels, and summarized with the help of AI. We do not control or verify third-party event organizers, platforms (such as Zoom or YouTube), or registration processes — please review any event's own registration page for how the event organizer handles your information.
